Zuppler Online Ordering Plugin enables customers to order food online directly from restaurant website.

== Description ==

**Zuppler Online Ordering Plugin lets customers order food online directly from a restaurant's website, for takeout, delivery, dine-in and catering.** Zuppler's state of the art online ordering technology can be customized to match the brand, look and feel of the restaurant website. Customers stay on the restaurant website during the entire online ordering process. 

**Zuppler is currently serving customers in USA & Canada.**

You can learn more about Zuppler Powered Online Ordering Menus and programs at [ZupplerWorks.com](http://zupplerWorks.com/)

For additional questions and comments email us directly at [tech@zuppler.com](mailto:tech@zuppler.com)


== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `zuppler-online-ordering` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

You will find 'Zuppler Online Ordering' menu in your WordPress admin panel.

= Testing the plugin =

1. Use the channel slug as `demorestaurant` and the restaurant slug as `site`
1. Create a new page and add the following shortcode somewhere in your content: `[zuppler menu=1]`
1. Visit the page and view the menu for our Demo Restaurant and how online ordering works.

If you like what you see, then you can proceed to

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How much does it cost to download the plugin =

The plugin is free for the wordpress developers. There are no charges to download and use the plugin.

= How much does it cost my restaurant client? =

There are no upfront fee for the restaurant. Setup and maintenance is on us. The restaurant will pay a service charge for orders that are generated online. You can find [more details about our pricing](http://zupplerworks.com/pricing-signup). There are also flat rate programs available for qualified restaurants.

= Does the restaurant need to sign a contract to use your service? =

There are no binding contracts to use our service. It is a pay as you go model. There are no cancellation penalty etc. You do need to sign up and agree to do business with us for us to configure and host the restaurant menu.

= How will restaurant receive orders placed from their website? =

Every order placed will be sent via fax/email/POS. Restaurants also have the option to view their orders instantly through their control panel. For fax and email notification, there will be an automated phone call to confirm the order. Zuppler's 24/7 customer service department monitor and handle orders that are not confirmed and conform to our "No Order Left Behind" policy.
